    Circle Visualization:
    
         ╭─────────────╮
        ╱               ╲
       │    85 / 100     │  ← Score displayed in center
       │                 │
        ╲               ╱
         ╰─────────────╯
          ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓░░░   ← 85% filled (green)

    Formula:
    ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │  Circle Circumference = 2 × π × 85 = 534 pixels                   │
    │  Filled Portion = Score × 5.34                                     │
    │  For Score 85: 85 × 5.34 = 453.9 pixels                           │
    │  Result: 453.9 / 534 = 85% filled                                  │
    └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

    Color Coding:
